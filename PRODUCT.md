# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Boda Boda Riders (Workers)**: Operates on mobile, often in outdoor conditions with high glare (sunlight), needing instant updates on their active coverage status and a quick premium payment path.
- **Hospital Admins**: Operates on desktops/tablets, verifying patient claims quickly and managing their API keys.

## Product Purpose
Provide a micro-insurance management dashboard for Boda Boda riders and partner hospitals, enabling riders to maintain daily coverage status via small, frequent payments and hospitals to verify patient claims in real time.

## Brand Personality
- **Three Words**: Trusted, Urgent, Kinetic
- **Voice/Tone**: Clear, reliable, and functional. Focuses on speed and absolute clarity.
- **Emotional Goals**: Foster security for riders (knowing their health is covered) and speed for hospital admins (saving administrative overhead).

## Anti-references
- Avoid the "AI SaaS" default template: soft purple-blue gradients, low-contrast text (e.g., light gray on white), and massive card grids.
- Avoid nested cards and excessively rounded borders (>16px).
- Avoid diagonal background stripes, grid-mesh overlays, or tiny uppercase kicked eyebrows above every single title.

## Design Principles
- **Outdoor Legibility**: Text must be high-contrast, highly readable under direct sunlight (minimum contrast ratios ≥ 4.5:1, preferring black/dark ink on clean light surfaces).
- **Temporal Urgency**: The coverage countdown timer must feel alive, driving riders to act before expiration but without creating false anxiety.
- **Frictionless Onboarding**: Workers can find their coverage status and pay premiums using only their phone number without requiring a complex login.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA level contrast ratio compliance.
- Support for reduced motion (e.g. static countdown updates or subtle pulsing instead of fast animations).
